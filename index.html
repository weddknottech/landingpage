FAQs

When will I get access?

We are releasing access to certain groups of members on a rolling basis to ensure that our users have an optimal experience before we launch publically. If it has been over a month since you've signed up, shoot us an email at support@weddknot.com.

Why should I request early access?

We're grateful for those that have been with us since day one. All early users will get exclusive coupons for free upgrades to our premium and plus plans and unique referral codes for further discounts!

Is the free forever plan permanent?

Of course! It is not a trial, and you can use the platform without losing data for free. Even after you run out of requests, you'll still be able to view other matches and decide if you'd like to upgrade or wait until your requests top up. Remember that you'll have unlimited incoming requests and be able to view details of all your matches even on the free forever plan.

Can't find your question?

No problem, we love helping people out. Visit our contact page, or any of our social media pages (in the footer) to contact us!
